Artist Bio

Cassandra Seal is a freelance artist located in Calgary, Canada. She works with a plethora of artistic mediums, ranging from oil paint to digital art tools. She loves exploring new project ideas and artistic modes.

Despite her quiet demeanour, Cassandra is arguably the loudest of her siblings. When she is not listening to true crime podcasts, Cassandra can be found reading webtoons or doodling caricatures. Not only does she appreciate art, but Cassandra also enjoys rainy days, and takes frequent walks during thunderstorms.
